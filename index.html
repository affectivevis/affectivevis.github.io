Affective Visualization Design: Leveraging the Emotional Power of Data
The website presents our study on affective visualization design, i.e., using data visualization design to communicate and influence emotion.

The "Research Corpus" page presents the corpus containing 109 academic publications we collected about affective visualization design. We coded these publications in terms of how they justified the legitimacy of considering emotion in visualization design to clarify WHY emotion is important for visualization.

The "Design Analysis" page presents 61 projects that practice affective visualization design. We coded the projects in three dimensions to explore the design space of affective visualization design, including their design fields (WHERE), design tasks (WHAT), and design methods (HOW).
