Affective Visualization Design: Leveraging the Pathos of Data
The website presents our study on affective visualization design, i.e., using data visualization design to communicate/influence emotion.

The "Research Corpus" tab presents the corpus containing 108 academic publications we collected about affective visualization design.

The "Design Analysis" tab presents 61 specific projects that explicitly practice affective visualization design, as well as our analysis on these projects, including their design field (Where), design tasks (What), and design methods (How).
