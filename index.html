The website presents the design methods we identified in the paper as well as the study material we used to evaluate these design methods. In the “Design Methods” tab, we present the 19 designed methods proposed by experts that have the potential to augment the communication of negativity. Each chard shows the name of the design method, a brief description, an example, the source of the example, and its "effectiveness ratio", which shows the percentage of the participants who thought this design method is effective in augmenting negative emotions in our user study. You can also click the "View comments" button to see why the participants thought the design method is / is not effective. The color of each card encodes the category of the design method.
